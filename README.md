#Supermarket Sales Analysis using SQL

## Project Overview
This project analyzes supermarket transactional data to extract meaningful business insights. 
The goal is to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and sales patterns using SQL queries.

---

## Objective
To analyze sales data and answer key business questions that help management in decision-making related to inventory, staffing, and revenue optimization.

---

## Tools Used
- MySQL
- CSV Dataset
- SQL Queries

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional sales records with the following key columns:

- Invoice ID
- Branch
- City
- Customer Type
- Gender
- Product Line
- Unit Price
- Quantity
- Total
- Date
- Time
- Payment
- Gross Income
- Rating

---

## Business Questions Solved

1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. Which branch generates the highest revenue?
3. Which product line sells the most?
4. What are the peak sales hours?
5. Which payment method is most preferred?
6. How do members vs normal customers compare in spending?

---

## Key SQL Concepts Used

- Aggregate Functions (SUM, COUNT, AVG)
- GROUP BY
- ORDER BY
- Date & Time Functions
- Filtering Conditions

---

## Key Findings

- Certain branches contribute significantly higher revenue than others.
- Evening hours show peak sales activity.
- Some product categories dominate total sales volume.
- Members tend to generate higher revenue compared to normal customers.
- Digital payment methods are widely used.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis provides actionable insights that can help in:
- Inventory management
- Staff allocation during peak hours
- Revenue optimization strategies
- Customer targeting
