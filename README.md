## Supermarket Sales Analysis Using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes supermarket sales transaction data using SQL to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance.
The goal is to extract meaningful business insights that can help improve sales strategy and operational efficiency.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall revenue performance
- Identify high-performing product categories
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Discover peak sales time during the day
- Evaluate payment method preferences

---

## Tools & Skills Used

- SQL
- Data Aggregation
- GROUP BY & ORDER BY
- CASE Statements
- Business Data Analysis

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains supermarket sales transactions with the following attributes:

- Invoice ID
- Branch
- City
- Customer Type
- Gender
- Product Line
- Unit Price
- Quantity
- Tax
- Total Sales
- Date
- Time
- Payment Method
- Rating

---

## Key Business Questions

1. What is the total revenue generated by the supermarket?
2. Which branch generates the highest revenue?
3. Which product line contributes the most to total sales?
4. Do member customers spend more than normal customers?
5. At what time of day do most sales occur?
6. Which payment method is most preferred by customers?

---

## Analysis Approach

The analysis was performed using SQL queries to:

- Aggregate revenue metrics
- Compare sales across different branches
- Identify top performing product categories
- Analyze customer segments
- Understand sales patterns throughout the day

---

## Key Findings

- Member customers generated slightly higher revenue compared to normal customers.
- Afternoon recorded the highest sales revenue among all time periods.
- E-wallet and cash were the most commonly used payment methods.
- Revenue contribution varies across branches and product categories.

---

## Business Impact

Insights from this analysis can help businesses:

- Improve staffing during peak sales hours
- Optimize product inventory based on demand
- Strengthen customer loyalty programs
- Encourage digital payment adoption
